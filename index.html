Crux Heroix - Red River Gorge November Trip 2023

Event Details

Where: Slade, KY
When: November 3-5, 2023

Event Description:

The Red River Gorge is located in the state of Kentucky. The Red is in the eastern part of the state, about 60 miles southeast of Lexington. Climbing here in the spring is great but there is a greater chance of rain than in the fall.

We will be staying at Land of the Arches (LOTA) campground . It is an “open site” campground with WIFI, restroom facilities and hot showers. However, there will not be access to a fridge and potable water. The most reliable time to charge your phone will be on your drive but there are outlets.

Climbing area (Muir Valley-RRGCC) Waiver: Muir Valley – Kentucky's Premier Climbing Destination.

All belaying will be done on assisted braking devices unless otherwise discussed with the Crux Heroix Admin crew.
